Mentoring Application

Time

September 2020 - November 2020

Role

UX / UI design

Team

Indivisual project

Overview

The Guider application project is my personal design project and Guider is designed to easily enable people to find their mentors or mentees. I understood pain points from the mentees' side and mentors’ side in order to make a better mentoring service.

Context

I’ve been always supporting gender equality and thinking how to make a better environment for everyone even if it's a small change. Then a question came up into my mind which is “Why is the gender gap still big in 2020?”. All began with the question and I started to conduct this project. My goal is to design for support.

Problem & Insight

"Why is the gender gap still big in the tech industry? How can we close the gender gap and bring diversity? "
